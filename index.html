CRYSTAL COLLECTOR

Each of the crystals has a certain value. You can find that value by clicking on each of them. You will be given a random number. Your goal is to use crystal value numbers to match the random number while combining all numbers pressed. If your total will exceed the random number, you will loose a point. By matching the random number, you will receive a point. GOOD LUCK!
